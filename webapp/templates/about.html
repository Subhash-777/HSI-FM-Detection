About HSIDetect

AI-powered foreign material detection for food and agricultural products.

⬡ The Model

LiteNet is a dual-branch neural network with 236K parameters trained on hyperspectral imagery (204 spectral bands, 400–1000nm).

  • SpectralBranch — 1D encoder with SE attention over 204 bands → 128-dim embedding
  • SpatialBranch — 2D CNN over 3×3 spatial patches → 64-dim embedding
  • FusionNet — MLP combining 192-dim fusion → binary output

📡 RGB → Hyperspectral Bridge

Since cameras capture 3-channel RGB, this app uses a Gaussian spectral basis to estimate 204-band reflectance from each pixel:

  • Blue channel → Gaussian centred at 450nm
  • Green channel → Gaussian centred at 550nm
  • Red channel → Gaussian centred at 650nm

Best results come from high-quality, well-lit images of food products.

🎯 Training Data

  • Synthetic pre-training: 4.87M samples (18% positive)
  • Real fine-tuning: 87,200 samples (HSI-AgriFoodAnomaly dataset)
  • Loss: Focal (α=0.75, γ=2.0) + Dice

🏃 How to Interpret Results

  • SAFE — No significant anomalies found
  • ANOMALY DETECTED — Foreign material signatures found
  • The heatmap shows probability intensity (black=low → red=high)
  • The overlay highlights suspected regions in red
